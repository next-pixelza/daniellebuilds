06 /FAQ

Questions worth answering upfront.

Do you only work with businesses in Cape Town?

Not at all. We're based in Cape Town, but the majority of our projects are delivered remotely for clients across the UK, North America, Australia and the rest of Africa. Every meeting, review and handoff is built around async-friendly, timezone-considerate workflows.

How long does a typical project take?

A custom website typically runs 3–6 weeks from discovery to launch. SEO campaigns are ongoing engagements, with the first measurable lift usually visible by month two. Software and SaaS builds vary by scope — we'll give you a fixed timeline before any work begins.

What's included in the free consultation?

A 30-minute working session where we audit your current site or systems, identify the highest-leverage fixes, and scope out exactly what a project with us would look like — timeline, deliverables and investment. No pressure, no generic sales deck.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. Every project includes a post-launch support window, and most clients move onto an ongoing retainer for ongoing SEO, iteration and feature work — which is reflected in our 98% client retention rate.
